DONATING BLOOD

What is the need?


According to the WHO, India suffers from an Annual Deficit of 2 million units, as only 1% of the Population Donates Blood each Year. It is a constant requirement for a regular supply of Blood by a sufficient number of healthy people; in order to ensure that Blood will always be available WHENEVER & WHEREVER it is needed.




Benefits:


A lot of Myths are present about Blood Donation, which are the reasons why Potential and Existing Donors show Resistance to Regularly Donate Blood.
However - what most people FAIL to realize is that Donating Blood has Physical and Psychological gains:
1] Improved Cardiovascular Health
2] Protection From Cancer
3] Lower Risk Of Heart Attack
........are just a few of them.




Every 2 seconds someone needs Blood! Since it can't be manufactured, there is NO substitute for Human Blood. To overcome this Scarcity, many Blood Drives are regularly conducted by many organizations. Blood donation is an Extremely Noble Deed....!!!!
